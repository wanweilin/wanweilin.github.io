This course provides an introduction to the internal operation of modern computer systems, covering topics such as data representation, assembly language, memory hierarchy, linking, and exceptional control flow.

As a Teaching Assistant, my responsibilities included:
*   **Lab Sessions**: Guiding students through system-level programming labs (in C and Assembly).
*   **Office Hours**: Holding weekly Q&A sessions to answer questions regarding course materials and assignments.
*   **Grading**: Evaluating assignments, projects, and exams.
